About

e-Cosmos

At e-Cosmos, we’re committed to supporting our trusted partners and clients worldwide by harnessing the power of digital technology. We provide cost-effective services, solutions, and consulting to empower our clients’ business processes. Since our establishment in 2004, with our headquarters located in Tampa, USA, e-Cosmos has been extending global reach and domestic support to a diverse range of organisations, from start-ups to Fortune 500 companies. We offer universal access to exceptional talent and IT innovations through various delivery models, reducing overall risk and enhancing outcomes to help our clients succeed in the global economy.
